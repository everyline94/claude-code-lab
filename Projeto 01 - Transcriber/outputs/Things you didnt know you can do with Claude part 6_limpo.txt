If you search for Pixel Agents and click on the first link, someone built a tool to watch your AI agents do all the work. When you install this tool, you'll be able to visualize all your agents working in a virtual office. Every agent is like one employee, and you will see a speech bubble if they're waiting for your input or need some permissions from you.

It's interesting to think that apps we used during the pandemic are now being used by agents, and we'll just watch them work.
